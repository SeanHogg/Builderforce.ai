# Website Audit: Benefit-Driven Messaging & CTA Enhancement

## Problem & Goal

**Problem:** Our current website messaging is generic and doesn't effectively communicate the specific value proposition of our product to our diverse Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) roles. This leads to low engagement, poor conversion rates, and a failure to attract and retain the right users.

**Goal:** To redesign website messaging and calls-to-action (CTAs) to be highly tailored and benefit-driven for each distinct ICP role. This will result in increased user understanding, higher engagement, improved trial sign-ups, and ultimately, a stronger pipeline of qualified leads.

## Target Users / ICP Roles

*   **Marketing Managers:** Focus on campaign performance, ROI, lead generation, brand visibility, and efficiency.
*   **Sales Leaders:** Concerned with pipeline growth, revenue targets, sales team productivity, deal closure rates, and competitive advantage.
*   **Product Managers:** Interested in feature adoption, user engagement, product innovation, customer feedback, and market differentiation.
*   **Customer Success Managers:** Prioritize customer retention, churn reduction, user satisfaction, onboarding efficiency, and customer lifetime value.

## Scope

*   **Messaging Review & Revision:** Audit existing website copy (homepage, product pages, feature pages, landing pages) to identify areas where messaging can be made more benefit-driven and ICP-specific.
*   **ICP Persona Mapping:** Clearly map revised messaging and CTAs to each ICP role.
*   **CTA Optimization:** Redesign and strategically place trial CTAs across the website, ensuring they are prominent, clear, and contextually relevant to the surrounding content and the visitor's likely ICP role.
*   **Content Hierarchy & Flow:** Ensure a logical and engaging flow of information that guides visitors towards understanding the product's value for them and ultimately, to a trial.
*   **A/B Testing Framework:** Establish a framework for A/B testing revised messaging and CTAs to measure their impact on key metrics.

## Functional Requirements

1.  **ICP-Specific Value Proposition Statements:** Develop and implement concise, benefit-driven statements for each ICP role that clearly articulate how our product solves their unique pain points and helps them achieve their goals.
2.  **Targeted Content Blocks:** Introduce new or revise existing content sections on key pages that directly address the needs and motivations of each ICP role.
3.  **Contextual CTA Placement:** Strategically place trial CTAs on relevant pages and within specific content sections where they will resonate most with the identified ICP visitor.
4.  **Clear & Compelling CTA Language:** Craft CTA button text that is action-oriented, benefit-driven, and clearly indicates the next step (e.g., "Start Your Free Trial," "See How [Benefit] with a Demo").
5.  **Prominent CTA Design:** Ensure CTAs are visually distinct and easily discoverable on the page, adhering to best practices for UI/UX design.
6.  **Messaging Consistency:** Maintain brand voice and messaging consistency across all revised website content.
7.  **Analytics Tracking:** Implement robust analytics tracking to measure engagement with new messaging and CTAs, and conversion rates for trial sign-ups.

## Acceptance Criteria

*   **Messaging Clarity:** Website copy clearly articulates specific benefits for each defined ICP role.
*   **CTA Visibility:** Trial CTAs are consistently visible and accessible on key pages relevant to each ICP role.
*   **CTA Relevance:** CTA placement and surrounding content are contextually relevant to the ICP visitor.
*   **Conversion Uplift:** A statistically significant increase in trial sign-up conversion rates is observed within 30 days of deployment, compared to baseline metrics.
*   **User Feedback:** Qualitative feedback from user testing or customer surveys indicates improved understanding of product value.
*   **Defined A/B Tests:** At least three A/B tests for CTA variations or messaging segments are defined and ready for execution post-launch.

## Out of Scope

*   **New Feature Development:** This project does not involve the creation of new product features.
*   **Complete Website Redesign:** While messaging and CTAs will be optimized, a full visual redesign of the website is out of scope.
*   **SEO Content Creation:** Creation of entirely new blog posts or extensive SEO-focused content is not included.
*   **Paid Advertising Copy:** This PRD focuses solely on website copy, not external advertising campaigns.
*   **Advanced Personalization:** Dynamic content personalization based on user behavior or deep segmentation beyond ICP roles is out of scope for this initial phase.
